Solshine is a small sacred space located in the heart of Patchogue village. Our staff is dedicated to the healing of mind, body and spirit, for inner peace, radiant health and the evolution of human consciousness. We wish to build a mindful and compassionate community with a passion for yoga and the healing arts, to nurture, support and empower one another on our journey.
